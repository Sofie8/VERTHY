Overview

Soil and phyllosphere microbiomes can boost crop resilience and reduce inputs. We identify, validate, and scale microbial functions that improve plant performance and support sustainable farming.

What we do

  • Genome mining & plasmidomics to discover functional traits (e.g. PGP, biocontrol, degradation).
  • Metabolomics to profile active compounds and mode of action.
  • Bench → field workflows: seed and foliar treatments, formulation, and stability testing.

Monitoring & verification

We monitor the degradation/growth-promotion potential of microbial communities, assess the presence and activity of target genes after inoculation (qPCR, amplicon/shotgun), and track introduced strains to confirm establishment and effect.

Outcomes

  • Shortlist of robust strains or consortia with validated field performance.
  • Formulation and success-control metrics for reliable on-farm use.
  • Scale-up plan: pilot design → grower trials → productization.
